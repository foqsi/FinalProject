Comparing Transportation

Transportation between the United States and Viet Nam are fairly similar.

Viet Nam

The main forms of transportation in Viet Nam are motorbikes, trains, and cars/trucks. There is also a large bus, plane, and ferry network throughout the country. The tickets for these are very cheap.
